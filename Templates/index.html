About Indira Canteen

The Indira Canteen was a subsidized food program launched by the Government of Karnataka to provide affordable and nutritious meals to the urban poor and low-income individuals. The canteens were named after India's former Prime Minister, Indira Gandhi. Canteen Management Software is an automated software which enables all the activities of the canteen and maintain a detailed account of food served at the canteen.. It allows secure and speedy transactions.
